Flamingo classes
----------------

The Flamingo toolbox model is based on a series of classes:

* :class:`FlamingoBase`
  
  * :class:`FlamingoImage`
  * :class:`FlamingoDataset`
  * :class:`FlamingoDatasetPartition`
  * :class:`FlamingoModel`
    
* :class:`FlamingoBlockchain`
* :class:`FlamingoBlockchainException`
* :class:`FlamingoConfig`
* :class:`FlamingoHash`
* :class:`FlamingoIO`
